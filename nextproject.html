Smart Bandages

At Inova Soin, we’re excited to announce our next groundbreaking project: an Advanced Sensor Patch for Chronic Diseases. Designed to revolutionize the management of conditions like diabetes and cardiovascular diseases, this innovative patch will monitor vital metrics such as blood glucose levels, heart rate, and blood pressure.

  • The data will be transmitted in real-time to a mobile app, providing patients and healthcare professionals with actionable insights to enable personalized and proactive care. This project reflects our commitment to pushing the boundaries of connected health technology.
